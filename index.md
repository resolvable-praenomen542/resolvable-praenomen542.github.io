## ⚙️ Initial setup

Once you open the app, you need to configure your settings. These steps ensure the AI hears your interview clearly.

1. Open the application from your start menu or desktop shortcut.
2. Select your primary microphone from the settings menu.
3. Check the box labeled Enable Real-time transcription.
4. Set the audio sensitivity level. We recommend starting at the middle setting.
5. Click Save to apply your preferences.

The software stays in your system tray while it runs. You can right-click the icon to change settings or close the program at any time.

## 💡 Best practices for interviews

While the tool provides answers, your delivery impacts the interview outcome. Follow these tips to use the assistant effectively:

- Test your audio before the interview starts. Use a recording app to check that your microphone input is clear.
- Do not read directly from the screen. Use the AI suggestions to summarize your thoughts rather than repeating words verbatim.
- Keep the app window small. Position it near your camera so your eyes remain centered.
- Use the clear button to wipe old suggestions if the conversation changes topics quickly.

## 🛡 Privacy and security

The tool handles your voice data carefully. It sends audio to the backend service only for the purpose of generating interview answers. No audio storage happens on your local device unless you specifically choose to record a session. The transcription data disappears after the application closes.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems, verify these items first:

- Microphone access: Windows sometimes blocks apps from using the microphone. Go to Settings, then Privacy & Security, then Microphone to ensure the application has permission to record.
- Internet connectivity: If you see a spinning icon, your connection might be slow. Wait a few seconds for the AI to receive data.
- Application updates: If the tool fails to start, go back to the download page and check for a newer version. Uninstall the old version through your computer settings before installing the update.
- System volume: High background noise makes it difficult for the software to transcribe accurately. Try to sit in a quiet room for your interview.
